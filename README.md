# 📊 Project 4: Interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on building an interactive dashboard to analyze an e-commerce sales dataset. The dashboard provides a clear overview of business performance by presenting key metrics, sales trends, product performance, and customer payment preferences. Interactive slicers allow users to filter the dashboard by **Year** and **Quarter**, making the analysis dynamic and user-friendly.

## 📂 Dataset Information

* **Total Records:** 1200
* **Dataset Type:** E-Commerce Sales Data

### Key Columns

* OrderID
* Product
* Quantity
* UnitPrice
* TotalPrice
* PaymentMethod
* OrderStatus
* ReferralSource
* Date

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

* 💰 Total Sales
* 📦 Total Orders
* 🛒 Products Sold
* 💳 Average Order Value

### Interactive Filters

* Year
* Quarter

### Charts Included

* Sales by Product
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Orders by Payment Method
* Revenue by Payment Method

## 🔍 Key Findings

* **Total Revenue:** ₹1,264,761.96
* **Total Orders:** 1200
* **Products Sold:** 3535
* **Average Order Value:** ₹1053.97
* Chair generated the highest sales revenue.
* Online payment was the most preferred payment method.
* Sales reached their highest point during June.
* The dashboard allows users to analyze sales performance across different years and quarters.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase promotional campaigns for high-performing products such as Chair and Printer.
* Encourage customers to use Online payment methods through offers and discounts.
* Focus marketing efforts on Instagram to attract more customers.
* Analyze the decline in sales after June and identify opportunities for improvement.
